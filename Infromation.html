About us
KSI & Randolph have been making waves in the music scene since their first collaboration in 2011. Now the two YouTube superstars take to the stage to perform new music and iconic hits at venues across the UK and Europe.

After closing out VidCon London with a live performance, YouTube megastar and seasoned boxer Olajide ‘KSI’ Olatunji is hitting the road in furtherance of his music career. Olatunji and frequent collaborator (and fellow YouTuber Randolph) will embark on the New Age Tour across the U.K. and Europe later this year. The tour will feature special guests at various stops, and the duo will perform both new and old hits, according to a release, including “Lamborghini” — which has amassed more than 100 million YouTube views to date.

Some of the main locations for the UK tour include:

17th May – Bournemouth

18th May – Portsmouth

19th May – Cardiff

23rd May Birmingham

24th May- Liverpool

29th May – Glasgow

30th May – Newcastle

Some of the European Tour Date include:

26th June – Stockholm

28th June – Copenhagen

30th June – Amsterdam

7th July – Berlin

More tour dates are to be expected to be announced later this year for a US tour.
